Bolt-ons

Common upgrades that improve performance without opening the engine.

Quick tips before you buy

  • Set a goal: sound, spool, cooling, or top-end power.
  • Plan supporting mods: some parts need tuning, clamps, or heat management.
  • Compare value: cost vs estimated horsepower gain (use the calculator).

Turbo Inlet

Improves airflow before the turbo. Often paired with an intake.

Cat-back Exhaust

Mainly sound + flow. Low risk, easy install.

High-flow Intake

Better breathing and turbo noise. Gains vary by platform.

Intercooler

Reduces heat soak and helps consistency, especially tuned cars.
